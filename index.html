Exploring the Tech Space Together

Spectra is a brand-new women's hackathon coming to the West Coast in July!

This is a unique event bringing together female college students and professionals from all around the world. Spectra offers participants a safe and inviting environment for women to experiment with technology at any experience level.

Every woman should have the opportunity to have an impact in computer science and strengthen her technical abilities. What are you waiting for? Come learn new skills, inspire one another, and build long-lasting connections at Spectra.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

For unanswered questions, shoot us an email at hello@sospectra.com

What is a hackathon?

In this case, the word "hack" describes how multiple technologies can be used together in new and creative ways. Teams will spend the weekend creating a website, mobile app, or hardware creation. The Spectra team believes that the most important aspect of a hackathon is the community it generates and the skills that attendees can take away with them at the end of the event.

What is the cost to attend?

There is no registration fee for this event. Hack space, WiFi access, food, drinks, and swag are all included.

Who can attend?

Anyone who identifies as female and is at least 18 years old can register to hack! If you're not 18, stay tuned for other events tailored to you.

What do I need to bring?

All you'll need is a valid photo ID (a student ID works), your laptop, and your charger. Also, a pillow and some toiletries never hurt anyone!
